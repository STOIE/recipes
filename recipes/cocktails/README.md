# Cocktails

## Pina Colada

### Ingredients

*qty per serve*

* 40ml white Rum (malibu
* 40ml cream of coconut (else, coconut cream)
* 40ml pineapple juice
* 2 pineapple chunks (no exact science here)
* 1 cup ice

### Method

1. Place all the ingredients into a blender and blend until smooth.
1. Drink.
