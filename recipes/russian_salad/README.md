# Russian Salad

### Ingredients

* 8 potatoes, diced 10mm cubes
* 5 carrots, diced 5mm cubes
* ~600 gram jar of pickles, diced 5mm cubes
* 500 grams peas
* 420 gram tin corn kernels
* 800 grams kewpie mayonnaise
* 200 grams sour cream
* 1 tablespoon dried oregano
* 1 tablespoon table salt
* 1 teaspoon cracked black pepper
* 1 lemon, juiced

### Method

1. Boil the potatoes and carrots in a pot of water without salt, until soft but not mushy (continuously checking) - adding peas (if frozen) about half-way through.
1. Once cooked, rinse in cold water to stop the cooking process.
1. Then add all the ingredients together in a large bowl and mix thoroughly.
1. Best kept in the fridge overnight before serving.
