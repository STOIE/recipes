# Pasta

## Dough

### Ingredients

*Scale as desired*

* 8 large eggs (6 whole, 2 as yolks only)
* 600 grams 00 flour ~12%

### Method

1. Place flour onto bench and make a well in the middle (or add everything to a mixer bowl).
1. If not using a mixer, place the eggs and yolks into the well in the middle and mix together with a fork until a dough is formed, then knead for 10 minutes minimum.
1. If using a mixer, attach a dough hook and mix on slow until incorporated then mix on high for 10 full minutes.
1. If the dough is still too sticky to handle add a bit more flour (max 50g per 600g).
1. Allow to rest covered in a bowl for 30 minutes before rolling.
1. When rolling use semolina flour as a lubricant.
