# Quiche

## Plain Quiche

#### Ingredients (makes one 9in pie dish)

* 65 grams (1/2 cup) self-rising flour (or plain plus 3/4 teaspoon baking powder)
* 60 grams (1/2 cup) cheddar/tasty cheese, grated
* 365 grams (1-1/2 cups) full-fat milk
* 100 grams (1/2 cup) rindless bacon, chopped
* 4 large eggs, beaten
* 1/2 medium onion (white or brown), very-finely diced
* 40 grams (1/4 cup) tomatoes, diced fine
* 1/4 teaspoon cracked pepper
* 1/4 teaspoon table salt

#### Method

* First, lightly fry the onion and bacon in a small frying pan over medium heat, then drain any excess oil and set aside.
* Next, in a large bowl, crack the eggs, add the milk and flour, then whisk everything through thoroughly.
* Then add the remaining ingredients into the bowl and mix again thoroughly.
* Pour into a standard (9in) pie dish and spread evenly over the surface.
* Place into a preheated oven at 180 deg C.
* Bake for approx 45 minutes, or until cooked through.
* Remove and allow to rest for 15 minutes before serving.


## Zucchini Quiche

#### Ingredients

* 375 grams (3 cups) course grated zucchini (ends removed, unpeeled)
* 160 grams (1 cup) cheddar cheese, grated
* 200 grams (1 cup) rindless bacon, chopped
* 120 grams (1 cup) self-rising flour (or plain plus 1-1/2 teaspoons baking powder)
* 5 large eggs, beaten
* 1 large onion (white or brown), very-finely diced
* 1/2 teaspoon table salt
* 60ml vegetable oil

#### Method

* In a large bowl, slowly add the flour into the eggs and mix well until combined without lumps.
* Then add the remaining ingredients into the bowl and mix through thoroughly.
* Pour into a ~700cm2 baking dish (such as a pyrex dish) and spread evenly over the surface.
* Place into a preheated oven at 180 deg C.
* Bake for approx 30-40 minutes, or until cooked through.
* Remove and allow to rest for 15 minutes before serving.
