## Panko Crumbing

### Chicken Schnitzel

#### Ingredients (6 full breast schnitzels)

* 4-5 eggs
* 1 cup plain flour
* 2 teaspoons table salt
* 1/4 teaspoon fine black pepper
* 2 cups panko crumbs

#### Method

1. Bowl one, combine flour, salt and pepper.
1. Bowl two, scramble the eggs.
1. Bowl three, add panko crumbs.
