# Rice Pudding

## Plain Original

#### Ingredients

* 350 grams white rice (jasmine)
* 700-1000ml water (depending on the rice)
* 1500-2000ml milk (depending on desired consistency)
* 1 vanilla pod (or 1 tablespoon vanilla essence)
* 1/2 teaspoon salt
* 100-200 grams sugar (depending on desired sweetness)
* 250ml thickened cream


#### Method

1. Place rice and salt into a pot with water on medium heat and cook until cooked through, not mushy though.
1. Once cooked, add in 1/2 the milk and the sugar, stirring on low heat until the mix thickens.
1. Add the remaining milk, vanilla and continue stirring on low heat.
1. Once again thickened (20-30 minutes), remove from heat and separate into individual cups/bowls.
1. Allow to fully cool before eating, however best served cold after being refridgerated overnight.

Note. can be served dusted with cinamon powder, or garnished with walnuts.
